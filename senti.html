Why Sentinel

Blazing-Fast Engine

How it Works

  1. Fast parse: Normalizes request path, headers, and body with zero-copy slices.
  2. Rule pipeline: Deterministic checks (regex-lite & token scanners) + heuristic scoring.
  3. Decision: Allow, block, or challenge with explainable reasons and audit trail.

Pluggable hooks let you add custom rules, telemetry exporters, and device-specific controls.

FAQ

What is it?+

Sentinel is a high-performance, embeddable WAF that filters malicious HTTP(S) requests before they reach your app or device.

How is it different?+

It’s tiny (~1 MB RAM), incredibly fast (10k req/s single-thread), and designed for both servers and constrained hardware.

License?+

Open-core: AGPL for the engine; optional paid features under commercial terms.

Can I use it on microcontrollers?+

Yes—fits within tight footprints and can run on extremely low-cost chips. OEM licensing available.

Is there a dashboard?+

Yes in Pro/Enterprise: real-time events, shadow-block view, alerts, and audit exports.
